# 📌 Project Overview

The Global Superstore Analysis project is designed to analyze retail business performance using Power BI dashboards and analytical storytelling techniques.

The project focuses on:

* Sales performance analysis
* Profit & loss tracking
* Customer behavior analysis
* Product demand analysis
* Order trend analysis
* Regional business performance
* KPI monitoring
* Business insights generation

A modern documentation website ( with the Help of Ai ) was also developed to professionally showcase the complete project structure, dashboards, KPIs, workflows, and insights.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

The project aims to answer important business questions such as:

* Which region generates the highest sales?
* Which category produces maximum profit?
* Which products receive the highest number of orders?
* Which countries generate losses?
* Which customer segment contributes most revenue?
* Which shipping mode is most frequently used?
* What is the company’s overall profit margin?

# 📌 Key Business Insights

## Orders Analysis Insights

* Office Supplies category generated the highest number of orders.
* Medium priority orders contributed majority of total orders.
* Central region recorded the highest order volume.
* Staples emerged as the most frequently ordered product.
* Consumer segment generated more than 50% of total orders.

---

## Sales & Profit Insights

* Technology category generated the highest overall profit.
* United States contributed highest net profit among all countries.
* Gross profit margin remained above 11%.
* Net loss accounted for nearly 38% of total gross profit.
* Revenue peaked during November and December.
